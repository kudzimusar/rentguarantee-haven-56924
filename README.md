# Proper Tier Properties - Zimbabwe Property Management Platform

## Executive Summary

Proper Tier Properties is a comprehensive property management platform designed specifically for the Zimbabwean real estate market. The platform revolutionizes property management by offering guaranteed rent payments, comprehensive tenant screening, and full-service property maintenance while eliminating vacancy risks for property owners.

## Business Overview

### Vision
To become Zimbabwe's leading property management platform, providing stress-free rental income for property owners while offering quality, affordable housing solutions for tenants.

### Mission
Transform the Zimbabwean property rental market by eliminating financial risks for property owners through guaranteed rent payments, comprehensive property management, and innovative technology solutions.

### Core Business
Proper Tier Properties operates as a full-service property management company in Zimbabwe, specializing in:
- Guaranteed monthly rent payments to property owners
- Comprehensive tenant screening and placement
- Property maintenance and condition guarantees
- Risk-free property management solutions
- Technology-driven rental optimization

## Market Analysis - Zimbabwe Focus

### Zimbabwe Real Estate Landscape

#### Market Characteristics
- **Urban Growth**: Rapid urbanization in Harare, Bulawayo, and other major cities
- **Housing Demand**: Growing middle class seeking quality rental properties
- **Currency Environment**: USD-based pricing provides stability for property owners
- **Market Challenges**: Traditional property management lacks reliability and owner protection

#### Target Cities
1. **Harare** - Capital city, largest market, high rental demand
2. **Bulawayo** - Second-largest city, industrial hub
3. **Chitungwiza** - Dormitory town with growing rental market
4. **Mutare** - Eastern border city, commercial center
5. **Gweru** - Central location, educational institutions

### Market Opportunity

#### Problem Statement
Zimbabwean property owners face significant challenges:
- **Vacancy Risks**: Loss of income during tenant turnover (average 2-3 months annually)
- **Tenant Defaults**: Payment delays and non-payment risks
- **Property Damage**: Lack of tenant accountability
- **Time Investment**: 6-8 hours monthly managing properties
- **Maintenance Issues**: Delayed repairs affecting property value

#### Solution Positioning
Proper Tier Properties eliminates these risks by:
- Guaranteeing full rent payment every month, regardless of vacancy
- Taking complete financial risk away from property owners
- Providing professional maintenance and property condition guarantees
- Reducing owner time commitment to less than 1 hour monthly

### Competitive Analysis

| Feature | Proper Tier | Traditional PMS | Self Management |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Income During Vacancies | Guaranteed | No income | No income |
| Financial Risk Bearer | Proper Tier | Owner | Owner |
| Owner Time Required | <1 hour/month | 3-5 hours | 6-8 hours |
| Property Condition | Guaranteed | No guarantee | No protection |
| Annual Net Returns* | $5,400 | $2,700 | $3,100 |

*Based on property with $500 monthly rent

## Target Market

### Primary Segments

#### 1. Property Owners (B2C - Primary)
**Demographics:**
- Age: 35-65 years
- Income: Middle to upper class ($2,000+ monthly household income)
- Location: Urban centers across Zimbabwe
- Property Value: $50,000 - $500,000 USD

**Psychographics:**
- Risk-averse investors seeking guaranteed income
- Time-constrained professionals
- Diaspora Zimbabweans with property in Zimbabwe
- Retirees relying on rental income
- Multi-property owners seeking professional management

**Pain Points:**
- Vacancy periods causing income loss
- Tenant payment defaults
- Property maintenance burden
- Time-intensive management
- Property damage concerns

#### 2. Tenants (B2C - Secondary)
**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-45 years
- Income: $400-$2,000 monthly
- Location: Urban areas in Zimbabwe
- Employment: Professionals, business owners, formal sector employees

**Psychographics:**
- Quality-seeking renters
- Value reliable property maintenance
- Prefer professional management
- Desire rewards and benefits programs

**Pain Points:**
- Finding quality rental properties
- Unreliable landlord maintenance response
- Lack of tenant protection
- No rental history building

### Market Size (Zimbabwe)

**Total Addressable Market (TAM):**
- Urban rental properties in Zimbabwe: ~200,000 units
- Average rental value: $300-600/month
- Market value: $60M - $120M annual rental market

**Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):**
- Properties suitable for guaranteed rent model: ~50,000 units
- Target market value: $18M - $30M annually

**Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM - Year 1-3):**
- Year 1: 200 properties ($1.2M annual rent managed)
- Year 2: 600 properties ($3.6M annual rent managed)
- Year 3: 1,500 properties ($9M annual rent managed)

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

#### 1. Management Fees (Primary)
- **Standard Package**: 15% of monthly rent
  - Guaranteed rent payment
  - Tenant screening and placement
  - Basic maintenance coordination
  - Monthly financial reporting

- **Premium Package**: 20% of monthly rent
  - All Standard features
  - Property condition guarantee
  - Priority maintenance response
  - Quarterly property inspections
  - Professional photography
  - Enhanced insurance coverage

#### 2. Setup Fees
- Property onboarding: $100-200 per property
- Initial property assessment and photography
- Legal documentation and agreements

#### 3. Tenant Membership Program
- Basic: $10/month - Maintenance priority, rewards
- Premium: $25/month - Extended benefits, insurance

#### 4. Ancillary Services
- Property maintenance markup: 10-15%
- Renovation project management: 15% fee
- Property valuation services: $50-150

### Cost Structure

#### Fixed Costs
- Platform development and maintenance
- Office space and utilities (Harare HQ)
- Core team salaries (management, operations, support)
- Insurance and legal compliance
- Marketing and brand development

#### Variable Costs
- Guaranteed rent payments to owners
- Maintenance and repairs
- Tenant acquisition costs
- Property inspection services
- Customer support scaling

### Unit Economics (Example)

**Property:** $500/month rent
- **Revenue:** $100/month (20% management fee)
- **Guaranteed Payment to Owner:** $400/month
- **Actual Tenant Rent Collected:** $500/month
- **Gross Margin:** $100/month
- **Variable Costs:** $20-30/month (maintenance reserve, support, inspection)
- **Contribution Margin:** $70-80/month per property

**Break-even:** 150-200 properties under management

### Database Schema (Planned)

```sql
-- Properties Table
properties (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  owner_id UUID REFERENCES users(id),
  address TEXT,
  city TEXT,
  neighborhood TEXT,
  property_type TEXT,
  bedrooms INTEGER,
  bathrooms INTEGER,
  size_sqm INTEGER,
  monthly_rent DECIMAL,
  status TEXT,
  created_at TIMESTAMP
)

-- Users Table (Owners & Tenants)
users (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  email TEXT UNIQUE,
  phone TEXT,
  user_type TEXT, -- 'owner' or 'tenant'
  created_at TIMESTAMP
)

-- Leases Table
leases (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  property_id UUID REFERENCES properties(id),
  tenant_id UUID REFERENCES users(id),
  start_date DATE,
  end_date DATE,
  monthly_rent DECIMAL,
  status TEXT
)

-- Payments Table
payments (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  lease_id UUID REFERENCES leases(id),
  amount DECIMAL,
  due_date DATE,
  paid_date DATE,
  status TEXT
)

-- Maintenance Requests
maintenance_requests (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  property_id UUID REFERENCES properties(id),
  description TEXT,
  priority TEXT,
  status TEXT,
  created_at TIMESTAMP
)
```

npm run dev

- User can select from Zimbabwe cities: Harare, Bulawayo, Chitungwiza, Mutare, Gweru
- User can input bedrooms (1-5+), bathrooms (1-4+), property size
- System provides rent estimate in USD within 3 seconds
- Confidence indicator shows prediction reliability (Low/Medium/High)
- Results include market comparison and recommendations

#### AC-2: ROI Calculator
- User inputs monthly rent in USD ($100-$5000 range)
- Calculator displays three comparison scenarios
- Charts visualize 12-month income projections
- All currency displays use $ symbol (USD)
- Assumptions section clearly explains calculations

#### AC-3: Property Listing Form
- Form collects: property type, location, size, bedrooms, bathrooms, amenities
- Photo upload supports multiple images (JPG, PNG, max 5MB each)
- Form validates all required fields before submission
- Success confirmation displays after submission
- Admin receives notification of new listing

### Business Rules

#### BR-1: Guaranteed Rent Calculation
- Owner receives 80% of market rent (Standard) or 75% (Premium with extra services)
- Payments made on 1st of every month, regardless of tenant occupancy
- Minimum property value: $50,000 USD
- Maximum guarantee period: 36 months per contract

#### BR-2: Property Eligibility
- Property must be in Zimbabwe urban areas (5 target cities)
- Property must pass initial inspection
- Market rent must be $200-$2000/month USD
- Property must have clear title/lease documentation

#### BR-3: Tenant Screening
- Credit check required for all tenants
- Employment verification mandatory
- Previous landlord references (minimum 2)
- Deposit requirement: 2 months rent

#### BR-4: Maintenance Responsibility
- **Standard Package**: Owner pays for repairs >$500
- **Premium Package**: Proper Tier covers up to $2000 annually
- Emergency repairs (<24 hour response): All packages
- Routine maintenance: Scheduled quarterly

#### BR-5: Contract Terms
- Minimum contract: 12 months
- Automatic renewal unless 60 days notice
- Early termination: 2 months penalty
- Rent review: Annual based on market conditions
